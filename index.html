How it works

Designed for your morning routine.

Open your dashboard, see what needs attention, move on. No training required. Live in under two weeks.

1

We connect to your existing systems

EduTrack pulls from your SIS, attendance register, and gradebook. No manual data entry. Compatible with CBSE, ICSE, and IB grade structures.

2

Your dashboard goes live in 48 hours

A single screen showing at-risk students, class-level trends, and pending interventions — updated every 24 hours and delivered to your inbox every morning.

3

Alerts surface before problems escalate

Automated flags fire when attendance drops below your threshold or grades decline across subjects — weeks before parent-teacher meetings.

4

Interventions are logged and tracked

Teachers record action taken. You can see which classes are improving, which interventions are working, and every parent communication — all documented.
